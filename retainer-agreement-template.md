## 1. The MetroGuard 15-Minute Guarantee
As a Ready-Response Partner, you agree to the **15-Minute Callback Guarantee**.
*   When a Tier 1 (Critical) lead is routed to you via SMS, you must contact the homeowner within 15 minutes.
*   If the callback is missed, the lead is automatically re-routed to the next available partner or the backup network.
*   Persistent missed callbacks (more than 3 per month) will result in a review of partnership status and potential downgrade to Tier 1 (Pay-Per-Lead).

## 2. Priority Routing
Ready-Response partners receive:
*   **First-Look Rights:** You receive the lead notification before any Pay-Per-Lead contractors in your territory.
*   **Intake Wizard Integration:** High-scoring leads from our AI Intake Wizard are sent directly to your mobile device via SMS.

## 3. Reporting & Fees
*   **Monthly Retainer:** Due on the 1st of every month via [Wave/Invoicing System].
*   **Success Fees:** Contractor agrees to report "Booked" jobs via the HubSpot Partner Portal or monthly audit.
*   **Lead Quality Disputes:** Any lead with a disconnected number or outside the agreed-upon service niche can be disputed for a credit toward next month's retainer.

## 4. Exclusive Territory
MetroGuard will not sign another Ready-Response partner for the primary ZIP codes assigned to you. We may, however, send "overflow" leads to Pay-Per-Lead partners if you are unavailable or non-responsive.
