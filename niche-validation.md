## Recommended Niche: Emergency Water Damage & Basement Waterproofing

### Why it wins in Metro Detroit:
1.  **Economic Resilience:** Water damage is usually an insurance-backed emergency. Homeowners don't "wait" to fix a flooded basement, ensuring high conversion rates regardless of the economy.
2.  **Infrastructure Issues:** Metro Detroit (especially Detroit, Dearborn, and Grosse Pointe) has aging combined sewer systems that frequently overflow during heavy rains, creating a recurring, predictable demand cycle.
3.  **High Ticket Value:** A typical water extraction and drying job starts at $2,500. Full waterproofing systems range from $5,000 to $15,000. This allows for high lead pricing ($150-$500/lead).
4.  **Low Barrier to Entry (Digital):** While physical restoration requires expensive equipment, a digital lead gen asset only requires a high-converting site and smart SEO.
5.  **Multi-Service Upsell:** Water damage leads are the "gateway" to mold remediation, foundation repair, and basement remodeling, making these leads extremely valuable to contractors.

### Data Points:
-   **Avg Job Value:** $3,500 - $12,000
-   **Target Lead Price:** $150 (Qualified Phone Call)
-   **Seasonality:** Peaks in Spring/Summer (Rain) and Winter (Pipe Bursts)
-   **Primary Competitors:** Belfor, Servpro (National), plus ~40 local Metro Detroit independents.
