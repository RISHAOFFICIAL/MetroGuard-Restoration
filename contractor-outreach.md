# Contractor Outreach Kit - MetroGuard Restoration

This document contains templates and scripts for reaching out to Metro Detroit water damage contractors to sell them exclusive leads generated by MetroGuard Restoration.

## Outreach Strategy: The "First 2 Leads Free" Offer
The goal is to lower the barrier to entry and prove lead quality before asking for a financial commitment.

---

## 1. Cold Email Template
**Subject:** Live Water Damage Leads for [Contractor Name] - Exclusive & Real-Time

**Body:**

Hi [Contractor Name],

My name is [Your Name] with MetroGuard Restoration.

We specialize in generating high-intent, exclusive emergency water damage and basement waterproofing leads specifically in the Metro Detroit area (Detroit, Dearborn, Warren, etc.).

Unlike HomeAdvisor or Angi, we don't sell the same lead to 5 different people. When a lead comes through our site, it goes **exclusively** to one partner.

I currently have a few live leads in [City] and I’m looking for one reliable local partner to handle them. 

**I’d like to send you your first 2 leads for FREE so you can see the quality for yourself.** No credit card required, no long-term contract.

If the leads are a good fit, we can talk about a simple pay-per-lead or monthly setup.

Are you available for a 2-minute chat this afternoon to see if this works for you?

Best regards,

[Your Name]
MetroGuard Restoration
[Your Phone Number]
metroguardmi.com

---

## 2. Cold Call / Voicemail Script

**The Hook:**
"Hi, is this [Contractor Name]? My name is [Your Name] from MetroGuard Restoration here in Detroit. I’m calling because I’m currently generating exclusive water damage leads in [City] and I need a reliable contractor to send them to. I’m not looking to sell you a subscription—I actually want to give you your first two leads for free just to prove they’re high quality. Do you have a minute to talk about how I can start sending these over?"

**If they ask about price:**
"Our goal is to keep it simple. After the first two free leads, we typically do a flat fee per qualified phone call—usually between $150 and $300 depending on the job type. But let's get you those first two first so you can see the ROI."

**If they are busy:**
"I completely understand. I’ll send you a quick text with my info. If you have an emergency opening later today, let me know and I’ll route the next call to you."
