# 30-Day Sprint — Path to First $2K Revenue

## Goal: Generate first $2,000 in revenue through local lead generation in Metro Detroit.

### Week 1: Foundation & Digital Asset Setup
- **Day 1:** Secure domain (e.g., MetroDetroitRestoration.com) and hosting.
- **Day 2:** Launch high-converting one-page site. Focus: 24/7 Emergency Response, Click-to-Call button, Trust Badges (BBB, IICRC).
- **Day 3:** Set up tracking phone number (CallRail/Twilio).
- **Day 4:** Create Google Business Profile (GBP) for a Metro Detroit location.
- **Day 5:** Set up basic CRM (HubSpot/Google Sheets) to track lead status.

### Week 2: Traffic Activation & Contractor Outreach
- **Day 8:** Launch Google Ads: "Emergency Water Damage Detroit" (Target 5-10 high-intent keywords). Budget: $50/day.
- **Day 9:** Identify 15 local restoration contractors in Metro Detroit with good reviews but poor websites.
- **Day 10-12:** Cold outreach (Phone/Email). **The Offer:** "I have live leads for water damage in Detroit right now. I'll send you the first 2 for free to prove quality."
- **Day 13:** Secure 3 "Proof of Concept" partners.

### Week 3: Lead Flow & Quality Assurance
- **Day 15-18:** Monitor inbound calls. Listen to recordings. Ensure partner contractors are answering the phone.
- **Day 19:** Qualify leads: Are they residential? Is it an active flood? Is it in our target area?
- **Day 20:** Follow up with partners: "How was the job? What was the quote amount?"
- **Day 21:** Lock in pricing: $150 per qualified lead or $1,500/mo retainer.

### Week 4: Monetization & Expansion
- **Day 22-24:** Present results to partners. Close first 2-3 contractors on a paid agreement.
- **Day 25:** Invoice for the first $1,000 - $2,000 (10 leads at $150 or 1 retainer).
- **Day 27:** Optimize Google Ads based on high-performing keywords/cities.
- **Day 30:** Review Month 1 results and set targets for Month 2 ($5K+).

### Priority vs. Defer
- **PRIORITIZE:** Phone call tracking, Google Ads, Contractor outreach.
- **DEFER:** Extensive blog content, social media profiles, fancy logo design, organic SEO (results take >30 days).
