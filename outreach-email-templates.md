## Quick Reference for Phone Calls

**Opening:** "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] with MetroGuard Restoration. I sent you an email about exclusive water damage leads — do you have 60 seconds?"

**If interested:** "Great. Here's what we do: homeowners call our emergency line or fill out our form. We capture their info, run it through our AI triage, and send the hot ones straight to your phone via SMS. You call them within 15 minutes while they're still in crisis mode."

**If objection "I already have leads":** "That's fine. But if you're getting shared leads from Angi or HomeAdvisor — you already know the close rate is low. Our leads are exclusive to your ZIP, pre-qualified with insurance and water data, and you get first look before anyone else."

**Close:** "First 2 leads are free. You don't pay anything unless you want to continue after that. Takes 5 minutes to set up — I can walk you through it right now if you're ready."
