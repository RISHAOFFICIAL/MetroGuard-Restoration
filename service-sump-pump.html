Don't Wait Until the Next Storm

Most basement floods happen during the worst storms — when your sump pump is most needed but most likely to fail. Power outages, frozen discharge lines, and worn-out pumps combine to create disaster at the worst possible time.

MetroGuard specializes in high-capacity sump pump systems and battery backup solutions that work even when the power goes out. We install only industrial-grade Zoeller and Liberty Pumps — the brands trusted by professionals worldwide.

⚠️
Storm Season WarningPower outages during storms are the #1 cause of basement flooding. A battery backup system can save you tens of thousands in water damage restoration costs.

Signs You Need a New Pump

  • Pump runs constantly or won't shut off
  • Strange noises or vibrating when running
  • Pump is more than 7-10 years old
  • Water pooling in basement during heavy rain
  • Pit overflows or pump can't keep up
  • No battery backup system installed
