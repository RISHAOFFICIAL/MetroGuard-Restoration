## 3. Internal Linking Plan
- **Service to City:** Every service page (Water Restoration) links to top city pages (Detroit, Dearborn).
- **City to Service:** Every city page links back to the main service categories for authority.
- **Blog to Service/City:** Blog posts link to relevant service pages (e.g., Sump Pump post links to Sump Pump Repair service page).
- **Homepage Hub:** Homepage links to all primary service and top 10 city pages.

## 4. Backlink Acquisition Ideas
- **Local Business Directories:** Metro Detroit Chamber of Commerce, local city business listings.
- **Guest Posts:** Reach out to Michigan real estate blogs and home renovation influencers.
- **News/PR:** Create a "Flood Watch" alert for local news stations when heavy rain is forecasted; provide "Basement Prep" tips.
- **Sponsorships:** Small sponsorships of local Metro Detroit community events or sports teams to gain `.org` or `.gov` local links.
