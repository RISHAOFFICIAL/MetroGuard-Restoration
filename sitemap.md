## 3. SEO & Content Strategy

### Local SEO Purpose
*   **City + Service Keywords:** Every city page will target `[City] Water Damage Restoration` and `Basement Waterproofing [City]`.
*   **Localized Content:** Mentioning local landmarks (e.g., "Serving homes near Ford Field" or "Waterproofing basements in the Woodward corridor") to increase local relevance for Google.

### Conversion Optimization (CRO)
*   **Floating CTA:** A persistent "Call Now" button on mobile.
*   **Sticky Header:** Form access always visible.
*   **Dynamic Phone Numbers:** Tracking numbers assigned per source (SEO vs. Ads) to measure ROI.

### Blog Strategy & Supplemental Monetization
The blog will focus on high-intent informational keywords to capture leads earlier in the funnel and generate supplemental revenue via AdSense.

*   **Content Pillars:**
    *   *Guide:* "What to do in the first 2 hours of a basement flood."
    *   *Cost:* "How much does basement waterproofing cost in Metro Detroit? (2024 Guide)"
    *   *Insurance:* "Does Michigan homeowners insurance cover sump pump failure?"
    *   *Prevention:* "5 signs your Troy home needs a new sump pump before the spring thaw."

*   **AdSense Monetization (Supplemental):**
    *   **Placement:** In-article ads only on blog posts. No ads on core service, city, or lead-capture pages to prioritize conversion.
    *   **Format:** Responsive Display and In-Article ads to maintain a premium feel.
    *   **Expected RPM:** $8–$20 in the home services niche.
    *   **Note:** Ad revenue is supplemental. The primary goal remains qualified lead generation for high-ticket service contracts.

## 5. Advanced Monetization Streams
Beyond primary lead generation and AdSense, the platform will utilize these five high-margin streams:

| Stream | Description | Revenue Potential | Automation Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Premium Lead Placement** | Top-of-page branding and priority lead routing for selected "Gold Tier" partners. | +$200-$500/mo per contractor | Needs some management (onboarding & partner relations) |
| **Insurance Advocate Referrals** | Referring complex claims to public adjusters or insurance advocates. | $100-$300 per claim lead | Fully hands-off (automated lead forwarding to partner CRM) |
| **Equipment Affiliate Commissions** | Affiliate links for DIY mitigation tools (dehumidifiers, sump pumps) within blog content. | 5-10% commission per sale | Fully hands-off (automated link tracking) |
| **Contractor Training Course** | Selling access to a "MetroGuard Certified" digital course on lead conversion for technicians. | $497-$997 one-time fee | Fully hands-off (automated LMS access) |
| **Related Trade Directory** | Monthly fees for vetted plumbers and electricians to be listed on "Partner" pages. | $50-$150/mo per listing | Fully hands-off (automated subscription billing) |
