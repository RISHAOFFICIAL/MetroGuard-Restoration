# MetroGuard Restoration: Operator's Manual
## Step-by-Step Launch & Revenue Guide

This manual provides a definitive walkthrough for launching and scaling MetroGuard Restoration. Follow these steps exactly to build your digital lead generation asset and hit your first $2,000 in revenue.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1–3)

### Day 1: Secure the Domain & Hosting
1.  **Register Domain:** Go to [Hostinger.com](https://hostinger.com) and buy `metroguardmi.com` (or your chosen alternative).
2.  **Point to Vercel:** 
    *   Set up a free account at [Vercel.com](https://vercel.com).
    *   Connect your GitHub repository (containing the site files) to Vercel.
    *   In Hostinger, update your Nameservers to point to Vercel (e.g., `ns1.vercel-dns.com`).
3.  **Launch Landing Page:** Deploy the `index.html` (Carrd-style) landing page immediately for a "Coming Soon" or "Emergency Help" presence.

### Day 2: Set Up Local Communications
1.  **Google Voice:**
    *   Go to [voice.google.com](https://voice.google.com).
    *   Claim a Detroit area code number (313, 586, or 248).
    *   **Greeting:** Record: *"Thank you for calling MetroGuard Restoration. All operators are currently assisting another caller. Please hold for the next available specialist."*
    *   **Auto-Reply SMS:** Enable SMS auto-reply: *"Thanks for calling MetroGuard! We'll have a specialist call you back within 5 minutes. For immediate emergency help, please visit our website."*
2.  **Forwarding:** Forward all calls to your personal mobile number so you never miss a lead call.

### Day 3: Initial Digital Assets
1.  **Email Setup:** Create a professional email (e.g., `dispatch@metroguardmi.com` or use a free Gmail for day 1).
2.  **GBP Verification:** Begin the Google Business Profile setup (see Phase 3).

---

## Phase 2: CRM & Automation (Days 4–7)

### Day 4: HubSpot CRM Setup
1.  **Create Account:** Sign up for [HubSpot Free CRM](https://hubspot.com).
2.  **Build Lead Pipeline:** 
    *   Go to **Settings > Objects > Deals > Pipelines**.
    *   Create "MetroGuard Lead Pipeline" with stages: *New Lead, Qualified, Dispatched, Contacted, Quoted, Closed-Won, Closed-Lost*.
3.  **Add Custom Properties:** Create contact properties for `lead_score`, `is_emergency`, `has_insurance`, and `urgency_tier`.

### Day 5: Lead Capture (Tally Forms)
1.  **Build Form:** Create a free account at [Tally.so](https://tally.so).
2.  **Create "Quote Request" Form:** Include Name, Phone, Email, ZIP, Service Type, Emergency (Y/N), and Insurance (Y/N).
3.  **Embed:** Add the form URL or embed code to your website's "Quote" page.

### Day 6: Automation (Make.com)
1.  **Connect Tally to HubSpot:**
    *   Create a free account at [Make.com](https://make.com).
    *   **Scenario:** Webhook (Tally) -> HubSpot (Create/Update Contact).
2.  **Build Routing Logic:**
    *   IF `is_emergency` = Yes -> Set `lead_score` to 150.
    *   IF `has_insurance` = Yes -> Add +50 to score.
3.  **Notification:** Set Make.com to email/SMS YOU immediately when a lead scores > 100.

### Day 7: Live Chat
1.  **HubSpot Conversations:**
    *   Go to **Conversations > Inbox** in HubSpot.
    *   Generate the free chat widget code and paste it into your website header.
    *   Set up a simple welcome message to capture visitor contact info instantly.

## Phase 3: Local Authority (Days 8–14)

### Day 8: Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization
1.  **Fill Profile:** Use the details in `gbp-setup.md`. 
    *   **Name:** MetroGuard Restoration
    *   **Category:** Water Damage Restoration Service
    *   **Hours:** Open 24 Hours
2.  **Service Areas:** Add Detroit, Dearborn, Warren, Troy, etc.
3.  **Verify:** Follow Google's verification process (usually a postcard to a local address).

### Day 10: Content & SEO
1.  **Post to GBP:** Upload 5 photos of restoration equipment and publish your first 3 keyword-rich posts (from the outreach kit).
2.  **Intake Wizard:** Ensure the Tally form is behaving as an "Emergency Intake Wizard" (multi-step feel) to increase conversion.

---

## Phase 4: Contractor Outreach (Week 3)

### The Strategy: "First 2 Leads Free"
Contractors are skeptical. We prove value first, then bill.

1.  **Identify Targets:** Find 15–20 local restoration contractors in Metro Detroit (Check Google Maps, Yelp, BBB).
2.  **Execution (Daily Tasks):**
    *   **Monday:** Send 10 cold emails using the template in `contractor-outreach.md`.
    *   **Tuesday:** Call the 10 people you emailed. Use the "Hook" script.
    *   **Wednesday:** Send SMS follow-ups to those who didn't answer.
    *   **Thursday:** Secure your first 2 "Proof of Concept" partners.
3.  **Pipeline Management:** Move interested contractors to the "Qualified" stage in your HubSpot partner pipeline.

---

## Phase 5: Monetization & Revenue (Week 4)

### Closing the Deal
Once you send a lead to a contractor and they confirm it was a real job, close the paid agreement.

1.  **Pricing Call:** 
    *   *"You saw the quality of those free leads. I'd like to lock this city in for you exclusively."*
    *   Offer **$150 per qualified call** or **$1,500/mo retainer**.
2.  **Agreement:** Use the `retainer-agreement-template.md` to send a simple 1-page agreement.
3.  **Invoicing (Wave):**
    *   Set up a free account at [WaveApps.com](https://waveapps.com).
    *   Send your first invoice immediately upon closing.
4.  **Tracking:** Mark the Deal as "Closed-Won" in HubSpot.

### Priority vs. Defer
*   **PRIORITIZE:** Answering the phone, Google Ads (if budget allows), and Contractor outreach.
*   **DEFER:** Social media posting, detailed blog writing, and logo design tweaks.
