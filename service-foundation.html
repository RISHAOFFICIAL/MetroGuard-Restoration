Foundation Problems Don't Fix Themselves

Foundation issues never get better on their own — they only get more expensive. What starts as a small hairline crack can quickly evolve into a structural failure that compromises the entire safety of your home. Early intervention is the key to minimizing costs and maximizing stability.

MetroGuard's foundation repair specialists are licensed structural contractors with decades of experience in Michigan soil conditions. We use only engineer-approved solutions and provide detailed reports for insurance and real estate documentation.

Warning Signs of Foundation Issues

  • Visible cracks in foundation walls or floors
  • Bowing or bulging basement walls
  • Doors and windows that stick or won't close
  • Gaps between walls and floors or ceilings
  • Uneven or sloping floors
  • Cracks in drywall, especially above doorways
  • Chimney leaning or separation from house

Our Foundation Solutions

Engineer-approved methods for every type of foundation problem

Pier & Beam Systems

Steel push piers or helical piers driven deep into stable soil strata to anchor your foundation to bedrock. The most effective solution for settling foundations in Michigan clay.

Wall Anchors

Steel wall anchors installed in the yard and connected to your foundation walls to counteract inward pressure and gradually straighten bowing walls over time.

Carbon Fiber Reinforcement

Ultra-strong carbon fiber straps bonded to your foundation walls for a permanent, nearly invisible reinforcement. Ideal for hairline cracks and minor bowing.

Epoxy & Polyurethane Injection

Structural epoxy fills and seals cracks permanently, restoring structural integrity. Polyurethane foam stops water infiltration through cracks and joints.
