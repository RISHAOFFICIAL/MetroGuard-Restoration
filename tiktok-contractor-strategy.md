# TikTok Contractor Lead Generation Strategy: MetroGuard Restoration

This strategy is designed to attract Metro Detroit water damage and restoration contractors to the MetroGuard platform. The goal is B2B lead generation: converting contractors into paying partners for our inbound homeowner leads.

---

## 1. Content Pillars (The "Why Follow?")

To attract contractors, we must speak their language and solve their biggest problem: **low-quality, shared leads from expensive national platforms.**

*   **Pillar 1: The Lead Quality Showdown**
    *   Compare MetroGuard's exclusive, real-time leads against "shared" leads from Angi/HomeAdvisor.
    *   Focus on the higher closing rate of exclusive calls.
*   **Pillar 2: Restoration ROI**
    *   Break down the math: "Spend $150 on a lead, close a $7,500 basement job."
    *   Showcase "Job of the Week" (with partner permission) highlighting project value.
*   **Pillar 3: Jobsite & Industry "Real Talk"**
    *   Quick tips on IICRC standards, moisture mapping, and equipment maintenance.
    *   Builds authority—contractors want to partner with someone who "gets" the business.
*   **Pillar 4: Detroit Built, Detroit Dedicated**
    *   Leverage local landmarks and weather events (heavy rains, pipe bursts).
    *   Position MetroGuard as the local "boots on the ground" marketing arm for Detroit businesses.

## 3. Lead Capture Strategy

**TikTok to Sign-up Flow:**
1.  **Bio Link:** Use a "Linktree" style page or a direct link to `metroguardmi.com/contractors`.
2.  **Lead Magnet:** Offer a "2024 Metro Detroit Restoration Pricing Guide" (PDF) in exchange for email/phone.
3.  **Comments:** Actively reply to contractor comments with "DM me 'LEADS' to see if your city is open."
4.  **In-Video CTA:** Always use a visual text overlay: "First 2 Leads FREE - Sign Up Now."
