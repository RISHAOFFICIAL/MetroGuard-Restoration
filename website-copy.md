### A. Emergency Water Extraction & Drying
**H1: 24/7 Emergency Water Extraction & Structural Drying**
**Intro:**
Water damage waits for no one. Whether it’s a burst pipe, a heavy storm, or a sump pump failure, standing water can cause catastrophic damage to your home’s structure and air quality within hours. MetroGuard Restoration provides rapid, professional water extraction and industrial-grade drying services to stop the damage in its tracks.

**Why Act Fast:**
Water is a progressive destroyer. Within 24 hours, drywall begins to swell, hardwood floors can warp, and mold spores begin to colonize. Immediate extraction and dehumidification are the only ways to prevent long-term structural issues and expensive secondary damage.

**Pricing Factors:**

*   The type of water (clean, grey, or black water).

### C. Sump Pump Installation & Repair
**H1: Reliable Sump Pump Repair & Backup Systems**
**Intro:**
Your sump pump is the heart of your home’s flood defense. In Metro Detroit, where heavy storms are common, a failing pump is an invitation for a disaster. MetroGuard specializes in the repair, installation, and maintenance of high-capacity sump pump systems and battery backup solutions.

**Why Act Fast:**
Most basement floods happen because of a simple pump failure or a power outage during a storm. Waiting until the next big rain to check your system is a gamble you can’t afford to lose. A proactive upgrade to a battery backup system can save you tens of thousands in restoration costs.

**Pricing Factors:**
*   The horsepower and capacity of the pump.
*   Complexity of the installation or discharge lines.
*   Battery backup system requirements.
*   Pit condition and sizing.

**Trust Signals:**
