When Water Strikes, Every Minute Counts

Water damage is a progressive destroyer. Within 24 hours, drywall begins to swell, hardwood floors can warp beyond repair, and mold spores begin to colonize in hidden spaces. What starts as a minor leak can quickly become a catastrophic structural failure requiring tens of thousands in repairs.

MetroGuard Restoration's emergency water extraction teams are standing by 24/7, fully equipped with industrial-grade extractors, air movers, and dehumidifiers. Our technicians arrive within 60 minutes — guaranteed — to begin the extraction process immediately, minimizing damage and maximizing what can be saved.

Using advanced moisture mapping technology, we identify every affected area — including hidden moisture behind walls and under flooring — to ensure complete drying and prevent future mold growth.

Our Extraction Process

  • Immediate water extraction using truck-mounted systems
  • Industrial-grade air movers and dehumidifiers
  • Thermal imaging to detect hidden moisture
  • Moisture mapping and documentation for insurance
  • Content manipulation and drying
  • Sanitization with hospital-grade antimicrobials
  • Final moisture verification and clearance

Water Damage Types

  • Category 1 — Clean Water: From broken pipes, sink overflows. Safest to remediate.
  • Category 2 — Grey Water: From washing machines, dishwashers. Contains contaminants.
  • Category 3 — Black Water: Sewage backups, flooding. Requires specialized handling.
