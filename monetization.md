# Monetization & Revenue Strategy: MetroGuard Restoration

This document details the revenue models and projected financial scenarios for the Metro Detroit local lead generation asset.

## 1. Monetization Models

### A. Pay-Per-Lead (PPL) - *Recommended for Startup Phase*
*   **Pricing:** $150 – $450 per qualified lead.
*   **Definition of "Qualified":** A phone call over 60 seconds or a form submission with valid contact info and a service need in Metro Detroit.
*   **Upside:** Easy to sell to contractors; zero risk for them.
*   **Downside:** Requires active tracking and billing.

### B. Monthly Retainer (Flat Fee) - *Best for Stability*
*   **Pricing:** $1,500 – $4,500 per month per contractor.
*   **Model:** We provide all leads from a specific territory (e.g., Oakland County) exclusively to one contractor.
*   **Upside:** Predictable recurring revenue; low administrative overhead.
*   **Downside:** Potential to leave money on the table if lead volume spikes.

### C. Revenue Share (Commission) - *Highest Potential*
*   **Pricing:** 10% – 15% of the total closed job value.
*   **Typical Job Size:** $3,000 (Extraction) to $25,000 (Full Waterproofing).
*   **Upside:** A single $15k job nets $1,500–$2,250.
*   **Downside:** Requires deep trust with the contractor and visibility into their CRM.

### D. Multi-Sourcing (Hybrid)
*   **Model:** Selling the same lead to 2-3 contractors at a lower price ($75-$100 each).
*   **Pros:** Maximizes revenue per lead.
*   **Cons:** Lowers conversion rate for contractors, potentially damaging long-term partnerships.

## 2. Projected Monthly Revenue Scenarios

Based on Metro Detroit search demand and the hybrid Tiered Partner model.

### Scenario 1: Conservative (Month 2-3)
*   **Partners:** 2 Tier-1 Contractors (PPL).
*   **Lead Volume:** 10 leads/mo at $200/lead.
*   **Monthly Revenue:** **$2,000**
*   **Status:** Initial proof of concept.

### Scenario 2: Realistic (Month 6-12)
*   **Partners:** 3 Tier-2 (Ready-Response) + 2 Tier-1.
*   **Fixed Revenue:** $1,200 (3 Retainers at $400).
*   **Variable Revenue:** 30 leads (Tier-2) + 10 leads (Tier-1) = ~$7,000.
*   **Success Fees:** 5 booked jobs at $100 = $500.
*   **Monthly Revenue:** **$8,700**
*   **Status:** Highly profitable digital asset.

### Scenario 3: Aggressive (Year 1+)
*   **Partners:** 10 Tier-2 (Ready-Response) covering all Metro Detroit ZIPs.
*   **Fixed Revenue:** $5,000 (10 Retainers at $500).
*   **Variable Revenue:** 100+ leads/mo (SEO + Ads) = $25,000.
*   **Success Fees:** 20 booked jobs at $100 = $2,000.
*   **Supplemental:** AdSense + Referral fees = $1,500.
*   **Monthly Revenue:** **$33,500**
*   **Status:** Market dominance.

## 4. Tiered Partner Model (Ready-Response Architecture)

| Feature | Tier 1: Base (Pay-Per-Lead) | Tier 2: Ready-Response (Retainer) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pricing** | $150 – $350 per qualified lead | $300 – $500/mo Retainer + $50-$100/job |
| **Commitment** | Pay-as-you-go | Monthly Subscription |
| **Territory** | Shared / Open | **Exclusive ZIP Code Protection** |
| **Lead Routing** | Best-effort (Shared) | **Priority "First-Look" SMS** |
| **Response Time** | No guarantee | **15-Minute Callback Guarantee** |
| **Qualification** | Standard Capture | **Intake Wizard Priority Triage** |
